Grazipix Chatbot - Embedding Instructions

To add the Grazipix chatbot to your website, simply copy and paste this code snippet just before the closing </body> tag of your webpage:


<script src="https://{{ request.host }}/static/js/embed.js"></script>

The chatbot will appear as a floating button in the bottom-right corner of your website. When clicked, it opens up the chat interface where customers can interact with the Grazipix Shopping Assistant.
