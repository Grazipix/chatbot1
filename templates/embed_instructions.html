Grazipix Chatbot - Integration Guide

1. Website Integration

Add this code to your website's HTML just before the closing </body> tag:


<script src="https://{{ request.host }}/static/js/embed.js"></script>

2. WhatsApp Business Integration

To integrate with WhatsApp Business:

  1. Set up a WhatsApp Business API account
  2. Use our chatbot endpoint: https://{{ request.host }}/get_recommendations
  3. Configure your WhatsApp webhook to forward messages to this endpoint
  4. Contact our support team for WhatsApp-specific integration assistance

3. Instagram Integration

For Instagram Business accounts:

  1. Set up an Instagram Business account
  2. Use the Facebook Messenger API to connect to our chatbot
  3. Configure your Instagram messaging webhook to use: https://{{ request.host }}/get_recommendations
  4. Contact our support for Instagram-specific integration details
